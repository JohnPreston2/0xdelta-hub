// How to Read the Dashboard — Forensic Metrics Guide

Don't know
where to look?

The dashboard shows 65+ forensic metrics per token. Here's how to read it in seconds: start with the big number, then check the danger signals, then the opportunities.

7.2
example
STEP 1 FHS — Forensic Health Score

The single number that summarizes everything. Aggregates liquidity, flow quality, holder distribution and technical momentum into a 0–10 score.

0–3 → AVOID 4–6 → CAUTION 7–10 → HEALTHY
⚠
danger
STEP 2 Check the 3 red flags
ICR
Impact Crash Risk
"If the biggest whale sells, how much does the price drop?" Computed from liquidity depth vs top wallet size.
>1.5 = CRITICAL
WCC
Whale Concentration
"How much supply is controlled by a few wallets?" Gini-like measure across top 20 holders.
>40% = RUG RISK
LCR
Liquidity Coverage Ratio
"Is there enough liquidity to handle a big sell-off?" Pool liquidity relative to market cap.
<2% = FRAGILE
◆
signal
STEP 3 Spot the opportunity signals
BPI
Breakout Potential Index
Multi-factor signal: volume surge + wallet accumulation + RSI divergence + Bollinger squeeze.
>1.5 = POTENTIAL MOVE
NBP
Net Buying Pressure
24h delta between buy and sell flows, normalized by volume. Detects accumulation vs distribution.
POSITIVE = ACCUMULATION
PHASE
Market Phase
What stage is this token at in its lifecycle?
EARLY_BREAKOUT ✓ ACCUMULATION ~ DISTRIBUTION ✗
⬡
deep
STEP 4 Deep forensic signals ADVANCED
SI
Squeeze Intensity
Bollinger band compression. Dual-timeframe: hourly + 15min. Extreme squeeze often precedes breakout.
>80% = EXTREME COMPRESSION
TCI
Team Concentration Index
Multi-signal: contract ratio, entity clustering, address prefix patterns, balance tier analysis.
>30 = INSIDER RISK
FCI
Flow Concentration Index
Unknown whale concentration + balance clustering among top holders.
>35 = CONCENTRATED FLOW
TL;DR
High FHS + low ICR + positive NBP + EARLY_BREAKOUT phase = the agent's ideal entry zone. That's exactly the signal the agent uses to trigger an autonomous swap. Everything else is context for deep analysis.
